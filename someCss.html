thoughts about rex

Rex orange county is a very underrated musician, but I like that about him. I went to see him in concert over the summer in Portland Oregon, and it was the best day of my life. He does sort of like alternative pop, and plays both the guitar and the piano.

I went to the concert with my friend Kaity, and Rex brought his girlfriend out on stage who also sings. She's really good!.
